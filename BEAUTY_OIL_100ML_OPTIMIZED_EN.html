Sweet Almond Oil with Vitamin E and Lavender is a natural plant-based cosmetic, obtained by cold pressing Italian sweet almonds. Enriched with vitamin E (tocopherol) with antioxidant action and lavender essential oil with calming and relaxing properties. Formulated without synthetic additives, it is suitable for all skin types, especially for dry, sensitive or dehydration-prone skin.

📋 How to Use

🧴

Daily Use - Body

Apply a small amount on damp skin after showering, massaging until completely absorbed.

🤰

Against Stretch Marks

Apply regularly on belly, hips, chest and thighs with circular movements.

👤

Face

Pour a few drops on a cotton pad to remove makeup and impurities; rinse with warm water.

💇

Hair

Distribute a few drops on the lengths before shampooing, leave on for 20-30 minutes and wash with a gentle shampoo.

💆

Massage

Use pure or as a base to dilute essential oils (1-2 drops per 10 ml of oil).
