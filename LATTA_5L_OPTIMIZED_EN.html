The territory between Ferla and Buccheri, in the heart of the Iblei Mountains, represents one of the most suitable olive-growing areas in Sicily. The olive tree grows on terraced calcareous and hilly soils, rich in biodiversity that contributes to the aromatic complexity of the oil.

🫒 Cultivars and Blend

Tonda Iblea

70%

Typical variety of the Iblei Mountains. Intense aromas of green tomato, fresh grass, artichoke and sweet almond.

Nocellara Etna

30%

Native to Mount Etna. Bold character with bitter and spicy notes that balance the sweetness of Tonda Iblea.
